Founder Application

Shiphouse is a 1–3 month founder residency for high-agency founders building serious companies.

We host selected founders across India, Southeast Asia, and South Asia, helping them with GTM, marketing, fundraising, investor access, and focused execution while they ship.

This is not a vacation or networking retreat. We are looking for founders with early traction, strong ambition, and the ability to move fast.

Best fit Live product, early users, or proof of demand
